About LeanBowl

At LeanBowl, we believe that hitting your protein goals should be both easy and delicious. Whether you're a vegetarian, vegan, or meat lover, our app helps you find the perfect recipes based on your desired protein intake.

Simply choose your preferred protein source (like paneer, tofu, chicken, eggs, and more), enter your daily protein goal, and get a list of customized recipes with accurate ingredient quantities to help you meet your target.

Why LeanBowl?
  • Personalized protein-based recipe suggestions
  • Scaled ingredient quantities to match your goals
  • Both veg and non-veg options
  • Simple, easy-to-follow recipes

Start your journey to smarter eating with LeanBowl!
